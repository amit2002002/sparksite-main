## ‚ú® Business Psychology Applied

1. **Psychological Pricing** - Prices end in 9s (14,999 vs 15,000)
2. **Decoy Effect** - Premium plan makes Standard look more valuable
3. **Social Proof** - "Most Popular" badge creates FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)
4. **Risk Reversal** - 30-day money-back guarantee removes purchase barrier
5. **Scarcity** - Limited-time offer ("Free domain + hosting for first year!")
6. **Loss Aversion** - "Free domain" appeals to value consciousness
7. **Authority** - Professional design builds credibility
8. **Clarity** - No technical jargon, benefits-focused copy
9. **Multiple Paths** - Different CTAs for different customer personas
10. **Reciprocity** - Free items (domain, hosting, consultation) build goodwill
